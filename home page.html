About Us

PROVIDING FAMILY TO THE NEEDY PAWS
Often when we are outside, we have seen how random stray animals slowly find their way to us. These poor creatures rely on humans usually for their survival as well as shelter. Only when we look into their eyes do we know the struggles they have to face every day.You can understand why it is important to adopt a stray that actually needs shelter and it would love you like no one else in the world. For those who want an excellent variety of dogs to choose from, there is nothing better than a shelter or a rescue center. For every one dog that you adopt from a rescue center or shelter, one more dog is taken in its place. So in a way you actually save two lives!
